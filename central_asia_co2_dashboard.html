Kazakhstan dominates the region at ~46% of total CA emissions, peaking at 286 Mt in 2013 before a sharp fall then partial recovery.
Turkmenistan has the highest per capita CO2 (~10.5 t) due to extensive gas flaring — despite being a far smaller total emitter than Kazakhstan.
Tajikistan and Kyrgyzstan are the region's smallest emitters — both rely heavily on hydropower, keeping per capita figures below 1.5 t.
